About Us

It all started when four childhood friends were bitten by a bug called “Jungle Safari” and dream to see a Tiger in its wild habitat. Then came the year 2012, and the dream came into reality with our first ‘Tadoba’ visit. Since then the this jungle fever has taken us to some of the exquisite and amusing wildlife sanctuaries and national parks such as Kanha, Ranthambore, Satpura, Pench and many more.

India is home to some of the unique flora and fauna species which make wildlife sanctuaries in India a paradise for nature and wildlife lovers. The hope of spotting a rare, wild animal in its natural habitat is the most exciting kind of hope. The feeling of taking a safari in these dense forests accommodating such rare wildlife species makes adrenaline rush through your blood.

This deep inner drive was the reason behind the idea of forming a group with a purpose to promote wildlife tourism and create awareness among commoners about the conservation efforts which is the need of the hour. ‘Stripes n Tales’ was established not only to organise jungle safaris but also to help local communities in terms of sustenance through it.

Who we are

‘Stripes n Tales’ is coinage of four of us which formed to venture into a ‘niche’ wildlife tourism industry and let everyone experience the beauty of Jungles in an organised way. Be it an amateur wildlife enthusiast Or a professional Wildlife Photographer..We are open to all!

Mission

To deliver mesmerizing jungle safaris accompanying knowledge of various flora and fauna to most common people around the globe.

Vision

To create awareness about wildlife conservation and endangered species besides helping local population in terms of livelihood and quality educations
