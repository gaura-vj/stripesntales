About US

Professional WildLife Tour Planner

It all started when four childhood friends were bitten by a bug called “Jungle Safari” and dream to see a Tiger in its wild habitat.

Then came the year 2012, and the dream came into reality with our first ‘Tadoba’ visit. Since then the this jungle fever has taken us to some of the exquisite and amusing wildlife sanctuaries and national parks such as Kanha, Ranthambore, Satpura, Pench and many more.
